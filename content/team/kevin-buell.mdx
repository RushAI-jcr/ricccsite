Research interests include critical care medicine, clinical informatics, and data-driven approaches to improving ICU patient outcomes. His work focuses on leveraging large-scale observational data and causal inference methods to understand how variations in care delivery affect patients in critical illness settings.

**Education**

- Pulmonary and Critical Care Fellowship, University of Chicago
- MS, Public Health Sciences for Clinical Professionals, University of Chicago
- Internal Medicine Residency, Vanderbilt University Medical Center
- MBBS, Imperial College London
